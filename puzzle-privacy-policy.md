# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 10, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how GRACQ ("GRACQ", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when merchants use the PuZzLe Shopify application ("PuZzLe" or the "Service").

PuZzLe helps merchants design and run geo-experiments to measure the causal impact of marketing investments.

We are committed to protecting personal data and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR").

## 3. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to merchants who install and use PuZzLe through Shopify.

PuZzLe is a business-to-business application. It is not intended for consumers or children.

## 4. Data We Collect

When a merchant installs PuZzLe, we may receive and process information made available through Shopify, including:

### Merchant Information

* Store name
* Store domain
* Shopify store identifier
* Merchant contact information provided by Shopify
* Subscription and installation information

### Order Information

Through Shopify permissions granted by the merchant, PuZzLe may access and process:

* Order identifiers
* Order status
* Order value and revenue information
* Order dates
* Shipping postal codes (ZIP codes or equivalent)
* Information necessary to perform geo-experiment measurement and analysis

PuZzLe only accesses data required to provide its services.

## 5. Purposes of Processing

We process data exclusively for the following purposes:

* Providing the PuZzLe service
* Running geo-experiments
* Measuring the causal impact of marketing investments
* Generating analytical reports and insights
* Maintaining, securing, and improving the Service
* Complying with legal obligations

We do not use data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

## 6. Legal Bases for Processing

Under Article 6 GDPR, we process personal data on the following legal bases:

### Performance of a Contract

Processing is necessary to provide the PuZzLe service requested by the merchant.

### Legitimate Interests

Processing may also be necessary for our legitimate interests, including:

* Ensuring platform security
* Maintaining service reliability
* Preventing misuse of the Service
* Improving the quality of our services

These interests are balanced against the rights and freedoms of affected individuals.

## 7. Data Hosting and Security

All PuZzLe data is hosted within the European Union using infrastructure provided by Scaleway.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal data against:

* Unauthorized access
* Accidental loss
* Destruction
* Alteration
* Disclosure

Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel with a legitimate business need.

## 8. Data Sharing

GRACQ does not sell personal data.

GRACQ does not share personal data with advertising networks, data brokers, or marketing platforms.

We do not disclose personal data to third parties except:

* When required by law;
* When necessary to comply with a legal obligation;
* When necessary to protect our rights or the security of the Service.

## 9. International Transfers

Personal data is hosted and processed within the European Union.

GRACQ does not intentionally transfer personal data outside the European Economic Area (EEA).

If such transfers become necessary in the future, we will implement appropriate safeguards as required by the GDPR.

## 10. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the Service and fulfill contractual obligations.

Merchant-related data is generally retained for the duration of the contractual relationship between GRACQ and the merchant.

When a merchant uninstalls PuZzLe or terminates the contractual relationship, associated data is deleted unless retention is required by applicable law.

## 11. Relationship with Merchants

In relation to order and customer-related data processed through Shopify, merchants are generally the data controllers and GRACQ acts as a data processor providing analytical services on their behalf.

Merchants remain responsible for:

* Ensuring they have a lawful basis for processing customer data;
* Providing appropriate privacy notices to their customers;
* Complying with applicable data protection laws.

## 12. Data Subject Rights

Under the GDPR, individuals may have the right to:

* Access their personal data;
* Request correction of inaccurate data;
* Request deletion of personal data;
* Restrict processing;
* Object to processing;
* Request data portability where applicable;
* Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Requests may be submitted to:

**Email:** [puzzle@gracq.eu](mailto:puzzle@gracq.eu)

We may request additional information to verify the identity of the requester before responding.

## 13. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

PuZzLe does not use:

* Advertising cookies;
* Analytics cookies;
* Marketing pixels;
* Behavioral tracking technologies.

Any technical cookies used are strictly necessary for the operation and security of the Service.

## 14. Children's Privacy

PuZzLe is designed exclusively for business users and is not intended for individuals under the age of 16.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any updates will be published through appropriate channels and reflected by the updated Effective Date at the top of this document.
